How to Play (Standard Insertion Sort)

1. The Key Card (yellow) is the one you are currently sorting.
2. Use Shift Left if the card to the left is larger than the Key.
3. Use Place Card once the card to the left is smaller (or you are at the start).
4. If you place it incorrectly, you lose a life!
5. Clear the whole array to finish the level.
