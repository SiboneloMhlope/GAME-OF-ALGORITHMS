How to Play

  • The yellow Key Card is the element you're currently sorting.
  • Press ⬅ Shift Left if the card to the left is larger than the Key.
  • Press ✓ Place Card once all cards to the left are smaller (or you're at index 0).
  • Wrong moves cost a life. Build consecutive-correct streaks to multiply your score!
  • Stumped? 💡 Hint shows the correct move for −30 pts.
